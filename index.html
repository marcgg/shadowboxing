Follow training sessions that will be different every time, such as:

Various modes

Focus on one mode, or mix them for a fully customized training session.

  • Pad Work will give you simple combos and freestyle combos to execute
  • Freestyle lets you do your own thing with optional directions
  • Repetition gives you one combo to practice for an entire round
  • Jump rope lets you practice your boxer skip and is a great way to boost your cardio
